Our Story

Founded in 2005, Strawberry Kids Playschool began with a simple vision: to create a nurturing environment where children could learn, grow, and discover their potential through play-based education. Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in early childhood education, having touched the lives of over 2000+ families.
