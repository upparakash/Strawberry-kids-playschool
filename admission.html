Frequently Asked Questions

When does admission open?

Admissions are open year-round, but we recommend applying early as spaces fill quickly. Priority enrollment for the new academic year begins in January.

Is there a waiting list?

Yes, if a program is full, we maintain a waiting list. Families are contacted in order as spaces become available.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a 1:6 ratio for toddlers, 1:8 for pre-nursery and nursery, and 1:10 for kindergarten to ensure individual attention.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes! We offer a 10% discount on tuition for the second child and 15% for the third child from the same family.

What is your refund policy?

Registration fees are non-refundable. Monthly fees are refundable with 30 days written notice. Please refer to our enrollment agreement for complete details.
